Azure and Cloud Computing Conference

All around the world user groups and communities will come together from April 23 - April 25, 2020 once again in the seventh Global Azure Bootcamp event!

As the Global Azure Bootcamp Linz has been growing over the last few years and reached about 200 attendees in 2019, we are happy to announce a new location for 2020 which will allow us to grow further - meet us at Hollywood Megaplex Pasching!

Join us on Friday, April 24, 2020!

Speakers

Internationally highly respected speakers will share their knowledge about topics around Azure and Cloud Computing.

Sponsors

Thanks to our great sponsors the entry will be free and we will be able to provide lunch, coffee, beverages and snacks.

Community

Organized by the community for the community. Exchange ideas with developers from all over Austria.

Call For Speakers

We are looking for insightful and practical talks, each lasting around 45 minutes. If you’re passionate about Azure and want to share your expertise with the community then we'd love to hear from you.
